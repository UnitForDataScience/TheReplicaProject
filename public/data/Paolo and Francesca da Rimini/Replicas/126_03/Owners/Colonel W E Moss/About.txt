Lt-Col W E Moss (William Edward Moss, 1874-1953) was an art and book collector. In 1937 he sold his collection, which included an important collection of William Blake, illuminated manuscripts, bindings for Grolier, Thomas Wotton, Matthew Parker, Count Mansfeld, and others, many reproduced, as well as paintings including some by Rossetti. His collection was sold in London through Sotheby's, 2-5 March 1937; 8-9 March 1937.

Caroline Ann Uhlman Compton (c. 1946-) is the daughter of Dr. Manfred Uhlman and Hon. Nancy Diana Joyce Croft. She married Albert Charles Compton in 1966. She and Albert Charles Compton were divorced in 1976. She lived in 2003 at 41 Downshire Hill, London, England. She was a trustee of the Uhlman Charity. She also collected art, including 17th century portraits she loaned to the Croft Castle, National Trust, and opened her private apartment there to the public to display the collection she inherited from her father.

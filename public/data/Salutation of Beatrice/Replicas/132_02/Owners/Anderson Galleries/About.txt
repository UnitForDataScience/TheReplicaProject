Anderson Galleries, Inc, 1887-1934, New York City, Park Avenue and 59th Street. In 1929, the American Art Association merged with the Anderson Auction Company to form the American Art Association-Anderson Galleries, Inc, and in 1938, the firm was taken over by Parke-Bernet Galleries, Inc., which had been formed a year earlier. Sotheby’s later purchased Parke-Bernet.

Victor Robert Penryn Monk Monck was born in 1910 at Rottingdon, Sussex, England.. He was the son of Major Charles Sydney Goldman and Hon. Agnes Mary Peel. He married Hon.Isolde Sheila Tower Butler, daughter of FitzWalter George Probyn Butler, 17th/27th Baron Dunboyne and Isolde Butler Tower, on 23 April 1949.  He died in 1987. He gained the rank of Commander in the Royal Navy Volunteer Reserve.   http://thepeerage.com/p27320.htm#i273200

Henry Beecham was born on 20 August 1888. He was the son of Sir Joseph Beecham, 1st Bt. and Josephine Burnett. He married Ethel Anne Baxter, daughter of Herbert Baxter, on 25 October 1914.2 He died on 10 January 1947 at age 58. He gained the rank of Lieutenant in the Royal Army Service Corps. http://www.thepeerage.com/p13709.htm#i137087

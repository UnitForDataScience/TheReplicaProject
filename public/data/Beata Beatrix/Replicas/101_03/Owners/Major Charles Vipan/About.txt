VIPAN, Major Charles, D.S.O. 1900; 3rd Batt. The Buffs; Hon. Captain in the Army; b.1894; 2nd s. of late Thomas Curtis Vipan of The Mansells, Beds; m. Mary Rances, 2nd d. of late Alfred Jones of Needingworth, Hunts, sister and co-heiress to late John Vipan Jones, also of Needingworth. Fellow of the Linnean Society. Served South Africa, 1900 (despatches, medal with clasps). Address: Ford Bank, St. Leonardo-on-Sea. Club: Junior United Services

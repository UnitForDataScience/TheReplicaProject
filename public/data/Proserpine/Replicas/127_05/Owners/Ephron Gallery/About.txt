Ephron Gallery was operated by Walter Ephron (d. 1972) at 123 East 57th Street, died last Thursday in Flower and Fifth Avenue Hospital. He was 77 years old. Mr. Ephron grew up in Vienna, where his father and an uncle operated art galleries. He later served in the Austrian Army in World War I. He lived in Paris and Vienna, and after the French defeat in 1940 made his way through Spain to the United States. He opened the Ephron Gallery soon afterward. (NYTImes obit, 12.19.1972, p. 46)

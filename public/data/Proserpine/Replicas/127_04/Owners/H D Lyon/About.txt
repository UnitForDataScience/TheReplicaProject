H. D. Lyon  (Harry Dexter Lyon; 1917-2004), London antiquarian bookseller. 
He was an extraordinary connoisseur who ran an international business from his small 18th-century house in South Kensington. It was a private place for rich, bibliophiles: a Georgian house that was Lyon's home for more than 50years. In his last decade he bad systematically run the stock down, selling most  of  the treasures   he  had bought.

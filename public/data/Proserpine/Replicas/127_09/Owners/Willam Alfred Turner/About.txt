William Alfred Turner (1839-1886), a Manchester cotton spinner and businessman, director for a time of the Edison Electric Lighting Company, who owned several paintings by Rossetti:  Joli cœur (1867 Manchester City Art Gallery), Water Willow, a chalk study for La Bella Mano, another one for Mnemosyne (1876, and the oil version of Proserpine (1877 priv coll). Through a sequence of other owners A Vision of Fiammetta is not in the collection of Lord Andrew Lloyd Webber.

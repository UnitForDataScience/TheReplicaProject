H. S. Sanders-Clark, shipowner, head of United National Colleries. Member of the Ranelagh Club (1900), Collector (owned works by John Brett), Cited in Christie, Manson and Woods, Porcelain and Objects of Art, Decorative Furniture, Eastern Rugs and Textiles: H. S. Sanders-Clark, I.A. Dessau, and Others (1936); Collection sold as H. S. Sanders Clark Collection, Sotheby's, London, July 30, 1936, lot 67. He was a member of the “A” Co., 5th Battalion in WWI. Catalogue of modern pictures and drawings, the property of H. S. Sanders-Clark, Esq., deceased: also ancient and modern pictures and drawings from other sources. London. 1936. 43 S.
